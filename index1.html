Crystal Collector

Good with numbers? Can you add things up quickly? Give CrystalCollector a try! Click on the colorful smoke image to the left. At the opening of the game, a goal number is selected at random, and it's your job to match that number by clicking on the crystals. Each crystal has it's OWN randomly picked number, and the goal is to make your crystal numbers add up to the same amount as the goal number. I would challenge you to start a stop watch, and see how quickly you can add up your crystals! Unsplash. Thank you for visiting our website. Please come again!

iCrypto

Do you invest in stocks? Ever heard of Crypto Currency?! This app is not only educational, but it's user friendly as well! In the new world of crypto currency, there are more than 6,000 currencies to watch, track, and invest in. We at iCrypto thought that was a bit overwhelming, and made it easier for you. With iCrypto, you can track only the coins that you want to track, and keep up to date with the top news article at the same time. Search the coins you want to watch, ans sit back ans watch your investments grow!
