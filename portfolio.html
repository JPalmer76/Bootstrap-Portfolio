Word Guess

Have a few minutes to spare? Click on the Word_Guess image to the left, and try out my Word-Guess-Game! Select a category and try your best to spell out the word. Becareful, you only get 10 wrong guesses! Just type the letter that you'd like to use, and the game does the rest. -Thank you in advance for trying my game! -JP

CrystalCollector

Good with numbers? Can you add things up quickly? Give CrystalCollector a try! Click on the colorful smoke image to the left. At the opening of the game, a goal number is selected at random, and it's your job to match that number by clicking on the crystals. Each crystal has it's OWN randomly picked number, and the goal is to make your crystal numbers add up to the same amount as the goal number. I would challenge you to start a stop watch, and see how quickly you can add up your crystals! I hope you enjoy! -JP
